My Research Interests

  • High Energy Astrophysics
    • Short Gamma Ray Bursts (GRB), which are borne from neutron star-neutron star mergers and neutron star-black hole mergers with recent focus on GRB170817.
    • Astrophysical jets and their luminosity.
    • Monte Carlo simulations modeling the angular dependence in observations of kilonovae from compact binary mergers.
  • Astrobiology
    • Using stochastic processes to computationally model the emergence of life from non-living rarified environments.
    • Chirality and autocatalysis
  • Cryosphere
    • Ice Dynamics and simulating the fate of Icelandic glaciers using regional climate models and PISM code
